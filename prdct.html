Madisonopoly

The memorable experiences in Langdon Street, the sleepless nights we had in College Library during finals week, that 2 AM Mac N Cheese slice at Ian's Pizza on Franklin, and many more.. How about buying those properties and renting it to your friends in a Monopoly game? You can build houses and hotels on your favorite Madtown spots. A token of your time spent in Madison or the token of new adventures :) The box comes with:
  • 32 red houses
  • 12 green hotels
  • 28 Title Deed Cards
  • 20.5K in game cash
  • 6 game tokens
  • 1 game box
  • 20 X 20" gameboard
